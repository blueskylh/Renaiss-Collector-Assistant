# Renaiss Artist Workflow

Before generating images, ask the user:

1. 想要什么角色？
2. 想要什么主题？
3. 风格：fantasy / anime / cyberpunk / retro TCG / minimal？
4. 是否加入稀有度文字？
5. 是否加入用户名或 wallet？
6. 是否沿用 Renaiss logo 的蓝、绿、黄、橙、红渐变？
7. 是否需要大面积空白方便打印后手绘？

Generate two files:

- Black-and-white printable line art.
- Full-color reference art.

The Renaiss logo from `assets/renaisslogo.jpg` must appear clearly in the card design as a crest, frame emblem, energy core, or badge.

Reminder to user:

> Renaiss Artist SBT 要求用户创作 Renaiss-related artwork，并在 X 上发布且 tag @renaissxyz。建议打印线稿后手动上色，并发布上色过程或前后对比。

Prompt seed, line art:

```text
Vertical TCG card illustration, clean black line art only, printable coloring page, original [character], Renaiss-inspired segmented logo clearly visible as a crest on the card frame, large blank areas for hand coloring, no grayscale, no filled colors, crisp outlines, collectible card border, fantasy trading card composition.
```

Prompt seed, color reference:

```text
Vertical TCG card illustration, full color reference, original [character], clear Renaiss segmented logo motif on the card frame and energy core, vibrant blue green yellow orange red gradient inspired by the Renaiss logo, polished collectible card art, dramatic lighting, premium trading card design.
```
